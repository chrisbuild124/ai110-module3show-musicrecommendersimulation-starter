# Profile Comparisons

## Late Night Focus vs. High-Energy Pop

Pretty much opposites — one wants calm lofi at low energy, the other wants high-energy pop. Both scored 0.97 at #1, just on totally different songs. The big gap between #1 and #2 in both cases (0.97 to ~0.71) shows how much the formula rewards a triple match on genre + mood + energy. When that song exists in the catalog, it runs away from the rest of the list.

## High-Energy Pop vs. Chill Acoustic

Pop had multiple good options competing; folk showed up once. The Chill Acoustic top result ("Spacewalk Thoughts") had no genre match — it won on energy and acousticness alone. That's the dataset imbalance showing up directly. Pop users got more variety, folk users basically got one real match and then a bunch of "close enough."

## Late Night Focus vs. Chill Acoustic

Both want low energy and acoustic sounds, so "Library Rain" shows up at #2 for both. The difference is Late Night Focus had two lofi songs to pull from, while Chill Acoustic only had one folk song. Similar taste profiles, but one got more targeted results just because the catalog happened to support it better.
